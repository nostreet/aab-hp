Welcome to All About Bees website.



We are a group of bee keepers, based in North Canterbury, who share a common interest and wish to either learn or assist others to learn good bee keeping practices.

Our mission statement:

To inspire and empower people, it is all about the bees

Our club was established in October 2016 and has a strong membership with a wealth of knowledge. Greame Narbey, is our club mentor and has been a beekeeper for over 55 years. He is a commercial bee keeper who is kind enough to assist us with training, knowledge and support.

We meet twice per month, club nights are on the third Monday of each month and hive days are the second Sunday of each month.

Club member ship is $30 per annum for a single membership and $50 for a family membership.

Please contact us to find out more about our membership, club nights and hive days, everyone is welcome to attend and we look forward to meeting you.
